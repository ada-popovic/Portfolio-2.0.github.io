The combination of my design expertise, a deep academic interest in media and technology, and a passion for storytelling allows me to develop creative and complex projects with a solid conceptual foundation.

My strengths include organising and connecting ideas, visually communicating complex information, and translating abstract concepts into clear, comprehensible explanations, whether through visualisations or writing.

EDUCATION

I graduated in Graphic Design from Royal Academy of Art in The Hague, where my graduation project Basketology won the Waag Technology & Society Award. I continued my education by joining two masters programmes at the University of Amsterdam (MA), where I graduated with distinction and specialised in critical analysis of creative generative AI.
