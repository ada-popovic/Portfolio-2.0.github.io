I am a designer turned scholar turned someone else.

I connect my design skillset with an academic interest in technology and penchant for humour / passion for X which allows me to create creative, complex and well thought-through projects, regardless of the discipline.

My understanding of the visual forms is paired with a fascination with linguistic storytelling, metaphors and the ways in which language structures our ways of thinking. Working with the content is to me just as important as working with the format. You can read some of my written works in the online reader.

As of August 2024 I am particularly interested in projects related to food design, restaurants, menus, hosting and general food culture.

Currently based in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

CV?
EDUCATION

I graduated in Graphic Design from Royal Academy of Art in The Hague, where my graduation project Basketology won the Waag Technology & Society Award. I continued my education by joining two masters programmes at the University of Amsterdam (MA), where I graduated with distinction and specialised in critical analysis of creative generative AI. For my MA New Media and Digital Culture thesis I wrote about perceptions of AI in online prompting communities. For my MA Philosophy thesis, I wrote about how generative AI changes our ways of seeing.
